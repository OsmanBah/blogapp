Her First

The Night I Loved Her All Over Again


It started out with the infatuation of her reflection. Her eyes pierced the mirror and beamed back a luminous glow. Her skin was black, fresh and tout. Her lips were full like a slice of orange. I knew I was already in love with her, but she loved herself a great deal more.

But that all seemed to change that night we sat down watching one punch man. Its a marvel watching her take in so much of my mindless entertainment. I enjoyed it more than she thinks. She did it for me and enjoyed it. It made me tell myself that I had to buck up. Its not everyday a guy ends up with gorgeous girl and she ends up being everything you asked for. Some guys aren't that lucky. But when I looked into those eyes of hers, I saw something. She was completely in my world. She looked happy there. Comfortable even. Then I felt her head on my chest. Her chuckles would tickle me as Saitama gives the Sea King another one of his famous blank stares. That's the night I loved her all over again.

Now Me:

When a man exceeds manhood he becomes a gentleman. This is the ultimate goal. There are rules governing the ascension.

They are:

  1. Always get your affairs sorted out. Never leave a mess.
  2. When on top, do not gloat. No one loves a soar winner.
  3. Always keep your word.

I am none of the above unfortunately. I try to be as dignified as possible. I try to have fun wherever I am. (Rule 9). But I think I have a long way to go to become a proper gentleman. I'm into things like movies, tech, art, fashion and architecture. My character and choices I make needs to improve.

A man once said - “If you want to change who you are, then change what you do.”
